About This Dashboard

34 key U.S. economic indicators across Housing, Labor, Rates, and GDP & Income — refreshed daily and served as a static site with no backend.

Data sources

  • FRED / St. Louis Fed — all 34 series
  • Freddie Mac — PMMS weekly mortgage rates
  • BLS — CPI, payrolls, unemployment
  • BEA — GDP, PCE, personal income
  • FHFA — House Price Index
  • NY Fed — SOFR

Update schedule

Weekdays at 8:30 AM ET · Thursdays at noon ET (PMMS)

Automated via GitHub Actions — no manual intervention needed.
